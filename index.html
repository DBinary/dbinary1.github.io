About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Lei Hu, currently an undergraduate student at USTB. I joined USTB from 2019 as an undergraduate student and will get my bachelor's degree in 2023. My current research is the development and application of the algorithm of single-cell multimodal omics data. I specialize on the study of the combination of machine learning and bioinformatics.

My interests and specialties are quite diverse, spanning from molecular biology, electronic engineering, machine learning, bioinformatcis and computer science. I am also hobbyist in Starcraft2, Battlefiled 2042, fitness and basketball.
